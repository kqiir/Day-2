A Manga that i am currently reading (Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo)

Jujutsu Kaisen ≈ Modulo is an official spin‑off/sequel manga to Jujutsu Kaisen written by Gege Akutami (the original creator of JJK) and illustrated by Yuji Iwasaki. It began serialization in Weekly Shōnen Jump in September 2025 and continues the world of Jujutsu sorcery about 68 years after the end of the original series’ main story, introducing new characters and conflicts in a future setting while expanding the lore beyond where the original left off.

The Prequel to the Series (Jujutsu Kaisen)

Jujutsu Kaisen is a manga series created by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who becomes involved in the world of curses and sorcery after swallowing a powerful cursed object. Akutami’s work is known for intense battles, dark themes, and deep character development.
